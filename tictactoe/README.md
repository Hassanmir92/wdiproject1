#TicTacToe (StreetFighter)

This is my first Ruby on Rails project produced on the 4th week of WDI at General Assembly.

The game comes with a street fighter theme playing the classic game of tic tac toe. 

###User Story
- User arrives onto the website and can only play and view profiles once he/she has registered.

![image](http://i.imgur.com/ItmWE1H.jpg)

- Once logged in they will be taken back to the home page where they have the option of clicking 'Play Now!'. They can then choose to play against other players or the computer.

![image](http://i.imgur.com/otMAtKI.jpg)

- The game begins. Each game is played alongside the result will be displayed on each of the users profiles.

![image](http://i.imgur.com/KrtKVyE.jpg)
